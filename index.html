About Me

Hey my name is Aashika Parekh. I'm so excited to be part of the Robotics club. In addition to robotics I'm a part of the Temptasian club and She's the First. I'm also a part of the Freshman Girls Volleyball Team, Varsity Girls Basketball Team, and Girls Spring Track and Field. Outside of school I also like to make, edit, and publish videos for my youtube channel. My other hobbies include skiing, surfing, travelling and exploring new places.




Take Away from this season

Just by being in a school that offers so many STEM clubs is amazing. I have found so many new friends and new talents this year. I'm able to build a working robot from scratch metal parts, drive the robot during pressured runs, scout and learn other teams strengths and weaknesses, and be able to work together with people outside of my comfort zone. I'm so happy that I was able to get the chance to be part of a diverse team and an exhilarating club.
